# Solar Panel Maintenance Guide

## Cleaning
Solar panels should be cleaned every 6 months to ensure maximum efficiency. Use water and a soft brush. Avoid abrasive materials.

## Troubleshooting
- **Low Voltage**: Check for shading or loose connections.
- **Inverter Error**: Restart the inverter. If error persists, check the manual.

## Safety
Always turn off the system before performing maintenance. Wear protective gear.
